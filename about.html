Our Story

Lifedent Pakistan

Lifedent Pakistan is a professionally emerging dental equipment and solutions company established with a strong vision to provide high-quality dental products and complete clinic solutions across Pakistan.

The foundation of Lifedent Pakistan is based on a long-term international partnership with Lifedent China, a well-established dental equipment manufacturing company that has been actively serving the global dental industry for many years.

Through this collaboration, Lifedent Pakistan has officially acquired the distribution rights of Lifedent China products in Pakistan, enabling us to bring advanced, reliable, and innovative dental solutions to the local market.

Lifedent Pakistan began its operations at the end of 2025, with a clear mission to modernize dental practices and improve healthcare standards by providing world-class dental technology locally.

Our Mission

  • Provide reliable and high-quality dental products across Pakistan
  • Build strong partnerships with international manufacturers
  • Support dental professionals with complete turnkey clinic solutions
  • Ensure excellent after-sales service and technical support
  • Promote modern dental technology in the local market

Our Vision

To become a leading and trusted dental solutions provider in Pakistan by delivering innovative, high-quality dental equipment and complete clinic solutions while contributing to the modernization of dentistry through advanced international technology.
